TRACABILITY OF GASPARD

CONTEXT

Being very interested in web design and ecology, I proposed to a French clothing brand to calculate the carbon footprint of their clothes and to implement on their website an information page on their ecological impact to help the customer to become aware of the environmental impact of the fashion world. The clothing brand GASPARD accepted with great enthusiasm.

PROCESS

With the help of extensive bibliographic research and information on the brand, I established an estimate of the carbon footprint of the manufacture of this brand's clothing. I then created a web page dedicated to informing the customer about the carbon impact of the garment they were consuming. This personal project taught me about responsible fashion, web design and how to run an ecological fashion company.
